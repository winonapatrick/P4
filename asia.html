My Trip to Japan

My week in Tokyo. Dec 7, 2021

Tokyo is one of the most exciting cities I have ever been to. It is an absolutely massive metropolis with so many exciting things to do.

I participated in the Tokyo food tour, which was an incredible introduction to the city. Our tour guide was born and raised in Tokyo, and was ready to share stories about the region, what it’s like to live there, traditions, history and of course plenty about the cuisine! I’d definitely recommend adding this to your Tokyo itinerary.

My Trip to India

Patrick Family Trip. Aug 20, 2016

If you have been reading my blog for a long time, you would know that I visit India every couple of years. I have a lot of family that lives all around India.

This trip, I visited my mom's side of the family in Mumbai. Mumbai is my favorite place to visit in India. It is a bustling, crowded metropolitan hub. Mumbai is a trading town, so if you love shopping like me, this is the place for you. The street markets in Mumbai are great places to shop for inexpensive, trendy clothes, and accessories. The street food is incredible as well, a must try!

My Trip to China

My week in China. Sept 20, 2015

China is a country full of rich history and culture, making it one of the most fascinating places in the world!

There is a spot in China for everyone. If you're a city person, there are bustling metropolises like Shanghai and Hong Kong to explore. If you love the outdoors, there are breathtaking natural sceneries like Zhangjiajie’s “Floating” Peaks. If you enjoy visiting historical sites, you'll love classic tourist spots like The Great Wall of China and the Forbidden City.
