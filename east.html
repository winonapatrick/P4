Explore the East Coast With Me

My Trip to Boston, MA

My weekend in Boston. Mar 7, 2022

Boston is a good city for a weekend getaway. I go there to visit my friend on campus every semester, and we're always able to experience something new.

Boston has a rich history and a legacy of arts, culture, and education. Boston has something for everyone. There are many diverse neighborhood with a myriad of great restaurants, museums, markets, and more.

My Trip to Baltimore, Maryland

Patrick Family Trip. May 20, 2021

Baltimore is a fun place where you can see street performers, visit trendy shops, explore interesting museums, and more.

It's a great family vacation spot. Baltimore’s Inner Harbor is a beautiful focal point of the city. Baltimore has over 200 neighborhoods, each with its own charm and experience.

My Trip to DC

My weekend in Washington, DC. Mar 20, 2021

Washington, DC is a cool place to visit if you are interested in historical sites and museums.

There is so much culture and beautiful neighborhoods to explore. Most of the popular museums and attractions are free, so the trip can be pretty budget-friendly.
